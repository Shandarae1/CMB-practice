Founded by brand new mom, Shanda Rae, in 2004 Custom Memory Book's mission is to help new moms find the perfect memory book for their precious little one.

Our memory books have allowed families to document all the joyful milestones their baby passes with beautiful custom books and pages made just for them. We hope you'll take a look around, connect with us, and join us while we all jot down our favorite memories.
